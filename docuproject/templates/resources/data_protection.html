Data Protection Policy

Last updated: July 30, 2025

At DocuNest, data protection is core to our services. This policy outlines how we ensure your data is securely stored, processed, and managed.

1. Data Storage
  • All files are stored in encrypted cloud infrastructure
  • Backups are performed daily and retained for disaster recovery
2. Access Control
  • Employees only access data needed to perform their duties
  • Two-factor authentication is enforced for internal admin access
3. Breach Response

In the event of a data breach, users will be notified within 72 hours and a full audit will be conducted.

4. Compliance

We comply with GDPR, Kenyan Data Protection Act, and other applicable data privacy laws.

Questions? Email us at security@docunest.com
